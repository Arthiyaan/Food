Drinks

    Fresh watermelon and basil combined with lemonade for a fruity and herb-infused summer drink.

  3. Iced Chai Latte




    Spiced chai tea mixed with milk and poured over ice, a sweet and aromatic iced beverage.

    Tequila, triple sec, and fresh lime juice shaken and served over ice, a timeless and zesty cocktail.

    Blended pineapple, coconut milk, and yogurt for a tropical and creamy smoothie, perfect for a quick and healthy refreshment.

  6. Cucumber Mint Cooler




    Crisp cucumber and mint muddled with gin and tonic water, a cool and revitalizing drink for hot days.
