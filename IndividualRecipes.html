Italian Recipes

1.Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients

  • 400g ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 1 celery stalk, finely chopped
  • 400g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 200ml red wine
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 400g spaghetti
  • Freshly grated Parmesan for serving

Cooking Instructions

  1. Step 1: In a large pan, brown the ground beef over medium heat.
  2. Step 2: Add onions, garlic, carrot, and celery. Sauté until vegetables are tender.
  3. Step 3: Pour in red wine and cook until it reduces by half.
  4. Step 4: Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 30-40 minutes.
  5. Step 5: Cook spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain.
  6. Step 6: Serve Bolognese sauce over spaghetti. Garnish with Parmesan.

2.Margherita Pizza

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • Fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Cooking Instructions

  1. Step 1: Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C)
  2. Step 2: Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface
  3. Step 3: Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Step 4: Arrange mozzarella slices on top. Add fresh basil leaves
  5. Step 5: Drizzle olive oil over the pizza and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Step 6: Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until crust is golden

3.Risotto ai Funghi (Mushroom Risotto)

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups Arborio rice
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth, warm
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Cooking Instructions

  1. Step 1: In a pan, sauté onions and garlic in butter until softened.
  2. Step 2: Add mushrooms and cook until browned.
  3. Step 2: Stir in Arborio rice and cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Step 2: Pour in wine and cook until it evaporates.
  5. Step 2: Begin adding warm broth one ladle at a time, stirring until absorbed
  6. Step 2: Continue adding broth until rice is creamy and cooked al dente.
  7. Step 2: Stir in Parmesan cheese. Season with salt and pepper

4.Tiramisu

Ingredients

  • 6 egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup mascarpone cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups strong brewed coffee, cooled
  • 1/4 cup coffee liqueur
  • 24-30 ladyfinger cookies
  • Cocoa powder for dusting

Cooking Instructions

  1. Step 1: In a bowl, whisk egg yolks and sugar until thick and pale.
  2. Step 2: Add mascarpone cheese and mix until smooth
  3. Step 3: In a shallow dish, combine coffee and coffee liqueur.
  4. Step 4: Dip ladyfingers into the coffee mixture and arrange a layer in a serving dish
  5. Step 5: Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers.
  6. Step 6: Repeat with another layer of dipped ladyfingers and the remaining mascarpone mixture.
  7. Step 7: Refrigerate for at least 4 hours. Dust with cocoa powder before serving.
